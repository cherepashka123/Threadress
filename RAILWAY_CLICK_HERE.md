# Railway - Where to Click

## You're Currently On: "Architecture" Tab

## What to Do:

1. **Click "Settings" tab** (in the header, all the way to the right, after "Logs")

2. **Scroll down** to find "Build & Deploy" section

3. **Set Root Directory** to: `server`

4. **Set Build Type** to: `Docker` (if you see this option)

5. **Click "Save"**

## That's it!

After saving, Railway will automatically redeploy with the correct configuration.
